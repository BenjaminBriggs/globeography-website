Support

If you’re experiencing an issue with Globeography or have feedback, we’d love to hear from you.

Contact us

The easiest way to reach us is by email:
support@globeography.com

Helpful information to include

  • Your device model (e.g. iPhone 14, iPad Air).
  • Your iOS / iPadOS version.
  • A brief description of what you were doing when the issue happened.
  • Screenshots, if relevant.

Data deletion requests

We don’t create accounts in the app. If you’ve emailed us for support and would like us to delete your email and any related diagnostics, just write to support@globeography.com and we’ll take care of it.
