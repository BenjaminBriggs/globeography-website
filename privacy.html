Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 November 2025

Globeography (“the App”) is developed by an individual developer (“we”, “us”). This page explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information we collect

  • Gameplay data (such as scores, progress, and settings) stored locally on your device.
  • Diagnostics (anonymous crash reports and aggregated usage analytics) provided by Apple if you have opted in to share analytics on your device.

How we use information

  • To operate and improve the App.
  • To fix bugs and understand which features are most useful.

Children’s privacy

Globeography is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected personal data from a child, please contact us via the Support page and we will delete it.

No ads or third‑party tracking

Globeography contains no advertising and does not use third‑party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not collect personal information from users of the App.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited diagnostic data may be processed by Apple in accordance with Apple’s privacy policies when you opt in to share analytics on your device. We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

Your choices

You can uninstall the App at any time. You may also be able to limit analytics collection via your device settings (for example, “Share iPhone & Watch Analytics” on iOS).

Data deletion

Globeography does not create user accounts. If you contact us for support, we will use your email address only to respond to your request. You can ask us to delete your support messages or any diagnostic data we may have received by emailing support@globeography.com. We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
